Full Stack Developer

Full Stack Developer with focus on Frontend Development.

With a background in Bioengineering and 3 years of professional Biotechnology experience in Drug Manufacturing, I had always thought that I had to follow that path. That's when I decided to take it into my own hands to change my path. With a 6 month Full Stack Developer Bootcamp and personal projects in building applications that I actually love. I know that the path ahead lies with many difficulties and unknowns but I find joy in always learning new things and applying them. Armed with a passion for design and making user friendly impactful applications, I embark on my journey as a developer.
